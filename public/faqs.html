Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HarvConnect

What is HarvConnect?▼
HarvConnect is a digital marketplace that connects Ghanaian smallholder farmers directly with buyers. Farmers can list their fresh produce, and buyers can browse, order, and have it delivered via registered transporters.
How do I sign up?▼
Click the "Get Started" button on any page. You can register as a Farmer (to sell produce), a Buyer (to purchase produce), or a Transporter (to deliver orders). Fill in your details and you're ready to go!
How do I list products as a farmer?▼
After logging in as a farmer, go to "My Products" in the navigation menu. Click "+ Add New Product", fill in the details (name, category, price, quantity, unit), optionally upload an image, and click "Add Product".
How does ordering work?▼
As a buyer, browse the Marketplace, click on a product, select your quantity, and click "Add to Cart". When ready, go to your Cart, enter your delivery address and Mobile Money payment details, then click "Place Order & Pay".
What payment methods are accepted?▼
We currently accept Mobile Money payments through MTN, Vodafone Cash, and AirtelTigo Money. Simply enter your phone number and select your network at checkout.
How is delivery arranged?▼
Once a farmer accepts your order and marks it "Ready for Transport", our system matches the nearest available registered transporter based on proximity. The transporter delivers the produce to your specified address.
Can I change my password?▼
Yes! Go to your Profile page and scroll to the "Change Password" section. Enter your current password to verify your identity, then enter your new password. A reset link will be sent to your email.
How do I become a transporter?▼
Click "Join as Transporter" in the footer and register with your vehicle type and capacity. Once registered, you can update your location from the Transport Dashboard and become available for deliveries in your area.
